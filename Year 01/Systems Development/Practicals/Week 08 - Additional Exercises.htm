Perl Exercises

Some of these exercises are look aheads - ie solution is provided in later programs. Try to do the exercise before reading next program.

Hint: Google Google Google Google Google Google Google Google Google .....

  • scalar1.pl
    • print a blank line between the 2 lines
  •  input.pl
    • read 2 lines - use the same variable ($want)
    • work out (by editing the code) what the chomp does.
  • match.pl
    • Why doesn't Book or BOOK get found? How can this be fixed using "/i"
    • Change the book for a holiday
  • string.pl
    • print in lowercase
    • more than 5 characters but less than 10?
  • loop1.pl
    • Does the foreach loop work with negative numbers?
    • What happens if you try to print out $i outside of the foreach loop?
  • file.pl
    • How can you only print out lines which contain the letter e. (See match.pl)
  • buy.pl
    • Make sure your program works for upper AND lower case (e.g. finds eggs or Eggs)
  • array.pl
    • use push to add another item on the list
    • what is the difference between @ and $ ? Why @shop but $shop[1] ? Hint: List or Scalar?
  • push.pl
    • What are these: pop, shift , unshift  - write example code.
  • hash.pl
    • Add another item to the hash list.
